Project Title: Customer Churn Analysis & Insights
A complete, client-ready exploratory data analysis (EDA) of a telecom churn dataset.

Dataset: Telco Customer Churn dataset (Kaggle, CC0 License)

Business Problem Statement:
A telecom company wants to understand why customers are leaving (churning) and what factors drive churn. The goal is to: - Identify high-risk customer groups - Extract actionable business insights - Provide recommendations to reduce churn

Business Objectives:
Perform complete data cleaning and validation
Conduct exploratory analysis to identify churn behaviour patterns
Visualize key trends clearly and professionally
Produce insights a business manager can act upon
