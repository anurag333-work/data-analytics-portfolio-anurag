**Project Overview:** <br>
A telecom company wants to understand why customers are leaving (churning) and what factors drive churn. This analysis explores churn behavior in the company using customer demographic data, service usage, subscription details, and billing information. The goal is to identify key churn drivers and provide actionable recommendations to reduce customer loss.

**Dataset Description:** <br>
- Total customers: 7,043
- Churned customers: 1,869
- Dataset features:
        ◦ Customer demographics
        ◦ Services subscribed
        ◦ Contract type
        ◦ Tenure (in months)
        ◦ Billing details (Monthly & Total Charges)

**Business Questions Answered:** <br>
- Analysis of churn behaviour patterns
- Identification high-risk customer groups 
- Extraction of actionable business insights 
- Recommendations to reduce churn
    
**Tech Stack:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

**Key Insights:** <br>
- 26.5% overall churn rate
- Fiber Optic users churn 2× more than DSL users
- Month-to-month customers show the highest churn
- Short-tenure customers are the highest-risk segment
- High monthly charges are strongly linked to churn
